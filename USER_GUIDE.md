# 🪷 MannMitra — Student User Guide

A private, AI wellbeing companion for India's exam aspirants. Everything below works on the web link — no install needed (though you *can* install it as an app).

**Live:** https://108tsrenukesh.github.io/MannMitra-AI/

---

## 1. First 60 seconds (onboarding)
1. Open the link. Pick **your exam** (NEET/JEE/CUET/CAT/GATE/UPSC/Other).
2. Pick **your language** — 22 Indian languages + English. The AI replies in your language.
3. Add a **nickname** (optional — you can stay fully anonymous).
4. Tap **Start**. That's it. No sign-up, no email.

## 2. The 5 tabs (bottom bar)

**📝 Check-in** — your daily heartbeat.
- Tap a **mood** (😣→😄) and/or write what's on your mind (a sentence is enough).
- Tap **Reflect with MannMitra** → the AI reads it and gives a short, warm reflection, the **hidden triggers** it spotted, a coping tip, and a **suggested exercise** (tap it to start).
- **🧠 Brain dump** button: pour out a messy rant → the AI sorts it into clear buckets (syllabus / rank / sleep…). It *organises*, it doesn't diagnose.

**💬 Talk** — a calm chat companion. Tap a starter chip or type. It's exam- and language-aware, gives coping strategies, and is **not** a therapist (it says so).

**📈 Patterns** — what your own check-ins reveal: check-in count, average mood, streak, a **mood-over-time** trend (hover a bar to see the exact **date & time**), a **mood distribution**, a **"pattern of the week"**, and a cloud of **what weighs on you**. Patterns appear after a few check-ins.

**🧰 Toolkit** — works fully **offline**: Box / 4-7-8 / Ocean breathing (animated), 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, muscle relaxation, thought reframes, a **Rank-anxiety reframe**, gratitude, and "reach out to someone."

**🎯 Exam** — set your exam date for a gentle countdown + stage-aware tips.

## 3. Safety — always one tap away
- The red **🆘 SOS · Talk to a human** button (top bar) opens real Indian helplines (Tele-MANAS 14416, iCall, Vandrevala, AASRA) with one-tap calling.
- If you ever write something that signals serious distress (in any tab), MannMitra **stops** and shows these helplines instead of chatting — this works even **offline**.
- **Safety Plan:** on the crisis screen (or Settings), create a private plan — your warning signs, what calms you, people to call, and your *reasons to keep going*. It's surfaced the moment you need it.

## 4. Your privacy (important)
- Everything — journals, moods, chat, safety plan — **stays in your browser on your device**. There is **no server and no account.**
- Only the text you choose to reflect on is sent to the AI (Google Gemini / Groq) to write back. Your words are **never used to train AI**.
- **Optional PIN lock** (Settings): set a 4-digit PIN so no one else on your phone can open it.
- **Export** your data or **Delete everything** anytime, in Settings.

> Because it's private and device-local: your history lives on the **same browser** you used. Use the same device/browser to keep your streak. (This is the trade-off for true privacy — no data ever leaves you.)

## 5. Turning on full AI (richer replies)
The app already works offline. For full AI-written reflections and chat, the hosted version has AI enabled. If you self-host, open **⚙️ Settings → AI keys** and paste a free **Gemini** (aistudio.google.com/apikey) and/or **Groq** key. Keys stay in your browser session only.

## 6. Install it like an app (optional)
- **Android/Chrome:** menu → "Install app" (or the address-bar install icon).
- **iPhone/Safari:** Share → "Add to Home Screen."
- **Desktop:** install icon in the address bar.
